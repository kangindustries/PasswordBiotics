How Strong Is Your Password?

PasswordBiotics

PasswordBiotics is a tool that checks your password strength, and estimates the cracking time for different attack scenarios. For security, this tool analyses your password locally and does not store what you type. As good Internet practice, do not enter real passwords anyway.

Interpreting the results

Crack-time results are only rough estimates and are never 100% accurate, as it depends on various factors, such as the attacker's hardware.

  • Seconds–minutes: Your password is weak and should not be used.
  • Hours–days: Your password possess some risks and can be improved or changed entirely.
  • Months–years: Your password is strong.
